About me

I'm a PhD Candidate at University of Milano-Bicocca, working in the MUDI (Modeling Uncertainty, Decisions and Interaction) lab, and PostDoc Researcher at the IRCCS Ospedale Galeazzi Sant'Ambrogio .

My main research interests are uncertainty management, machine learning, medical AI and human-AI interaction, as well as their intersection. Therefore, I'm primarily interested in (and authored publications related to):
  • Weakly supervised learning
  • Cautious inference, uncertainty quantification and conformal prediction
  • Soft clustering
  • Applications of ML and uncertainty modeling in medicine
  • Human-AI interaction and explainable AI in clinical decision-making
I'm also interested (in no particular order) in:
  • Formalisms for representing uncertainty (rough sets, fuzzy sets, belief functions, imprecise probabilities)
  • Theoretical computer science (esp. dynamical systems, logics, algorithm design, learning theory/theoretical ML)
  • Robustness, data reliability and robust ML
I really believe in the co-operative nature of science (and mathematics), and thus I'm always open to collaborations or simply to chat: if you want, you can contact me via Twitter or email (see left column!).
