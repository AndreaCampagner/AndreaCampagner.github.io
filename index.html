About me

I'm a PhD Candidate at University of Milano-Bicocca, working in the MUDI (Modeling Uncertainty, Decisions and Interaction) lab.

My main research interests are the representation and management of uncertainty and machine learning, as well as their intersection. Therefore, I'm primarily interested in:
  • Formalisms for representing uncertainty (incl. rough and fuzzy sets, belief functions, imprecise probabilities)
  • Weakly supervised learning
  • Cautious inference and conformal prediction
  • Soft clustering
  • Learning theory and theoretical ML
I'm also interested (in no particular order) in:
  • Medical AI/ML
  • Human-AI interaction and explainable AI
  • Theoretical computer science (esp. dynamical systems, logics and algorithm design)
  • Robustness and robust ML
I really believe in the co-operative nature of science (and mathematics), and thus I'm always open to collaborations or simply to chat: if you want, you can contact me via Twitter or email (see left column!).
