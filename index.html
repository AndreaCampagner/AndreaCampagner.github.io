About me

I'm a third year PhD student at University of Milano-Bicocca, working in the MUDI (Modeling Uncertainty, Decisions and Interaction) lab!
My main research interests regard the representation and management of uncertainty, and machine learning as well as their intersection. Therefore I'm interested in:
  • Mathematical formalisms for representing uncertainty (probabilities, rough sets, three-way decision, fuzzy sets, belief functions, ...)
  • Machine and statistical learning algorithms (esp. ensemble methods)
  • Learning theory
  • Conformal and cautious prediction
  • Weakly supervised learning
I'm also interested in:
  • Applications of AI in medicine
  • Human-AI interaction
  • Socio-technical implications of AI
  • Theoretical computer science (esp. dynamical systems and nature-inspired computation)
