Grants

ACM SIGCHI Gary Marsden Award

April 2023

I have been selected as one of the recipients of Gary Marsden Travel Award, awarded by the ACM SIGCHI society, which consisted of a full waiver of the registration fees at the CHI 2023 conference, as well as reimbursement of travel expenses. Grant assignment was decided based upon a curriculum vitae and motivations evaluation.

2022 BFAS Grant

Oct 2022

Awards

EurAI Dissertation Award

Oct 2024

I have been awarded the Best Dissertation Award by the European Association for Artificial Intelligence (EurAI) for my PhD thesis "Robust Learning Methods for Imprecise Data and Cautious Inference", which was recognized as being the best PhD thesis on Artificial Intelligence in Europe. The award was presented at the ECAI 2024 conference.
