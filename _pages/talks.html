Awards

Best Student Paper at IJCRS 2021

Sep 2021

My article (with Davide Ciucci), titled "Feature Reduction and Disambiguation in Learning from Fuzzy Labels using Rough Sets" won the Best Student Paper award at the 2021 International Joint Conference on Rough Sets.

Best Student Paper at IEEE CBMS 2021

Jun 2021

My article (with Lorenzo Famiglini, Giorgio Bini, Anna Carobene and Federico Cabitza) , titled "Prediction of ICU admission for COVID-19 patients: a Machine Learning approach based on Complete Blood Count data" won the Best Student Paper award at the 2021 IEEE Computer-based Medical Systems conference.

Best Presenter at ACM ICMHI 2021

May 2021

I won the Best Presenter award at the ACM 5th International Conference on Medical and Health Informatics, for my article (with Valentina Cavosi and Federico Cabitza) titled "Assessing the impact of medical AI: a survey of physicians' perceptions".
