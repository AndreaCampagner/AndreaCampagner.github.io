Education

PhD in Computer Science, University of Milano-Bicocca

Start: 2019 - End: 2023

The main subject of my PhD regards machine learning under uncertainty focusing on weakly supervised learning and cautious prediction both from an algorithmic/theoretical as well as experimental point of view. During my PhD I also collaborated with different hospitals in Italy (IRCCS Istituto Ortopedico Galeazzi, IRCCS Ospedale San Raffaele) and abroad, on medical machine learning projects, also applying the above mentioned techniques.

Visiting Student, Heudiasyc, Université de Compiègne

Start: Sep 2021 - End: Dec 2021

During my PhD I spent 3 months as a visiting student at the Heudiasyc research lab, under the supervision of prof. Thierry Denoeux. During this period, my main research projects regarded studying the relationships between two important uncertainty representation theories (rough set theory and belief functions) as well as their applications in knowledge representation and machine learning, especially in clustering.

Master of Science in Computer Science, University of Milano-Bicocca

Final Vote: 110 cum laude
Start: 2015 - End: 2017

The subject of my thesis regard the study of an algebraic model of uncertainty (orthopairs) and its relationships with other uncertainty models. In particular, I was interested in studying the generalization of information theory to this setting, as well as the applications of these ideas in the setting of clustering.
My studies were mostly focused on theoretical computer science and artificial intelligence. The main courses I followed regarded: machine learning, probabilistic graphical models, representation of uncertainty, computational complexity theory, non-classical logics, information theory, high-performance computing, dynamical systems theory

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, University of Milano-Bicocca

Final Vote: 110 cum laude
Start: 2012 - End: 2015
